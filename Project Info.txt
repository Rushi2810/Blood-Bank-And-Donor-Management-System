The BLOOD BANK AND DONOR MANAGEMENT SYSTEM is great project. this project is designed for successful completion of a project on blood bank management system. The basic building aim is to provide blood donation service to the city recently.
Blood Bank And Donor Management System (BBADMS) is a Web-based application that is designed to store, process, retrieve and analyze information concerned with the administrative and inventory management within a blood bank.
This project aims at maintaining all the information pertaining to blood donors, different blood groups available in each blood bank and help them manage in a better way.
